Chandelier Chamber of Commerce

Site Name

Chandelier Chamber of Commerce. The name reperesents is a fictional city the chamber operates in.

Site Purpose

The Chandelier Chamber of Commerce provides a hub for economic growth within the great city of Chandelier. We promote local businesses by hosting community events, hiring opportunities, and driving economic development through membership services and pushing for improved public policy.

Scenarios

  • What community events are happening this month?
  • What are the membership benefits?
  • What has been the economic development in this city over the past year?

Color Scheme

The site will use shades of black, gold, white to represent Chandelier's wealth and optimism.

Typography

The site will use Monsterrat for all text.
