About Us

Founded in 2005 in the vibrant city of Carlsbad, California, Bountiful Foods emerged as a local haven for health enthusiasts and proponents of sustainable living. What began as a modest market blossomed into a community staple, with a mission to champion local farmers and provide consumers with a diverse range of fresh, organic products.

In 2008, the company expanded its footprint, introducing a Community-Supported Agriculture (CSA) program to forge direct connections between farmers and consumers. Bountiful Foods gained acclaim for its commitment to supporting local agriculture and, in 2012, launched its own brand of organic products, solidifying its stance as a leader in quality and transparency.

In 2018, Bountiful Foods expanded its offerings with the introduction of a drink café within its flagship store, offering patrons a space to enjoy organic beverages. After seeing the popularity within the community, fostering a culture of nutritious and mindful eating.

Today, Bountiful Foods stands as a flourishing organic market and café, deeply intertwined with the fabric of Carlsbad. With a focus on wellness, sustainability, and community engagement, the company continues to evolve, embodying the principles of a nourishing and environmentally conscious lifestyle.
